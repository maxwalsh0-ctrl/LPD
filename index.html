About

Born here. Still here.

I'm Carl, and I've been painting and decorating Liverpool homes since 1992. It's just me on the job, I quote every piece of work in person and I'm on every site myself, so there's no call centre and no rotating subcontracted crew turning up at your front door.

I've worked all over the city: Victorian terraces in Aigburth, sash-window restorations in Mossley Hill, full house refreshes in Crosby and Formby, landlord turnarounds in the city centre. The common thread is always the prep, taping, filling, sanding and priming before I open a single tin. That's the bit that decides whether a finish lasts a year or ten.

I'm independently vetted by Checkatrade and fully insured. Quotes are free and no-pressure, anywhere in Liverpool and Merseyside.
